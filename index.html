Privacy Policy

EchoNote – Simple • Private • Offline

📌 Overview

EchoNote is a voice recording application designed to work entirely offline.

🔒 Data Collection

EchoNote does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

🎤 Microphone Usage

The microphone is used only when you start a recording. All audio stays on your device.

💾 Data Storage

All recordings are saved locally on your device. No cloud, no servers, no sharing.

🌐 Internet Access

EchoNote works fully offline and does not require an internet connection.

👶 Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

🔐 Security

All data remains on your device and is never accessible by third parties.

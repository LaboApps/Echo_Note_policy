Privacy Policy – EchoNote

EchoNote is a voice recording application designed to work entirely offline.

Data Collection

EchoNote does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Microphone Usage

The application uses the microphone only to enable voice recording when the user activates the recording feature. All recordings remain exclusively on the user’s device.

Data Storage

All audio recordings are stored locally on the user’s device. No data is ever sent to external servers.

Internet Access

EchoNote works fully offline and does not require or use an internet connection.

Children’s Privacy

EchoNote does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13.

Security

All data remains on the user’s device and is not accessible by any third party through the application.
